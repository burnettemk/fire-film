 * Spacing pattern
 *
 *  Simple Grid:
 *    Possible Pixel Spacings:
 *      [2, 4, 6, 8, 10, ..., 64, ..., 100]
 *
 *    Margin set to Auto to center it in page (page is the context)
 *      2560px - 128px
 *      1440px - 72px
 *      1024px - 51.2px
 *      768px - 38.4px
 *      425px - 21.275px

 *      320px - 16px
